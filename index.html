Game Rules

  • Pictionary: A picture is displayed on the screen, so you have to pass it to the mentioned person in order to make them guess what he is seeing without speaking, just by miming.

  • Dares: There’s a list of dares you should do otherwise you lose.

  • Tell The Truth: There’s a list of questions you should answer “honestly” otherwise you lose.

  • Guess the song: A song title will be shown on the screen, you have to pass it to the mentioned player in order to make the group guess the song without speaking, just by miming.

Tips

  • The difficulty is set to the dirty level of the game accordingly.
  • If a player looses in any category, you can make him drink a sip of alcohol, in other terms turn the Drinkover into a drinking game.
